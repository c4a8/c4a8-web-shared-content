<p>In our three-day assessment workshop, we prepare your company for the deployment of Microsoft 365 Copilot. We begin with a thorough review of your requirements to ensure that your organization is ready for this technology. Next, we explain the features of the Copilot and demonstrate how it can enhance your workflows. Through use cases tailored to your company, you will gain practical insights into using the Copilot in your specific environment. This workshop serves not only as training but as a strategic roadmap for the successful integration of Microsoft 365 Copilot and the full exploitation of its potential.</p>

<h2>Contents</h2>

<ul>
  <li>A detailed introduction to the basic requirements and license terms necessary for using Copilot</li>
  <li>Explanation of the specific requirements that must be met in order to successfully integrate Copilot into the Microsoft 365 ecosystem</li>
  <li>Discussion of the importance of compliance with data management and security policies and regulations</li>
  <li>A comprehensive presentation of how Copilot works, including its AI-based mechanisms and processes</li>
  <li>Highlighting the opportunities presented by the use of advanced artificial intelligence and how this technology can transform industries</li>
  <li>Development and discussion of specific use cases where Copilot can be of value to your organization in terms of operational efficiency and innovation</li>
  <li>Select and describe in detail one or two key scenarios from your business operations that demonstrate how Copilot can add significant value to your business</li>
</ul>
